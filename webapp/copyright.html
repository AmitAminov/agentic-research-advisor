Copyright & content policy

This public dashboard publishes aggregate numbers only about a private research-reproduction pipeline covering Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning.

  • No paper PDFs, no full paper text, and no publisher- or author-owned figures are hosted here. Those artifacts live only in the private working corpus used for the reproduction work itself.
  • The numbers shown (papers per area, reproduction outcomes, topic counts) are facts about our own pipeline and contain no copyrighted expression.
  • Where individual works are ever referenced, they are referenced by title and a link to the official source (e.g. the arXiv abstract page), never by rehosted content.
  • All summaries, metrics, code, figures, and animations produced by this project are original works of the pipeline, derived from our own reproduction runs; reproduced figures are our re-computations, not copies of the originals.
  • Papers are harvested exclusively through official public APIs (arXiv, Semantic Scholar, OpenAlex, Crossref) under their terms of service.

If you believe any content here infringes your rights, contact mldsaidlagents@gmail.com and it will be reviewed and removed promptly.
